About

Iannetti Studies is an independent research project focused on documenting structural patterns found within historical symbolism, mathematics, and timekeeping traditions. Only minimal information is shared publicly — such as DOI‑registered publications, Zenodo archives, and a verified Google Scholar profile — to establish authorship, timestamping, and academic record.

The core research remains intentionally withheld. No diagrams, system logic, or interpretive mechanisms are publicly shared. Full materials will only be accessible under academic review, legal protection, or formal institutional agreement.
