The first ROSETTA WHEEL™ printed proof sets are being prepared through Iannetti Studies.

ROSETTA WHEEL™ is a comparative symbolic systems framework focused on hidden structure, circular geometry, gates, mirrors, turns, number, and recurring organization across symbolic systems.

This first printed set is a public starting point. It introduces the framework without disclosing the full research archive, complete mappings, unpublished correspondence materials, or private working files.

What is included

Item One

Overview Brochure

A concise printed introduction to the ROSETTA WHEEL™ framework and its symbolic-systems scope.

Item Two

Public Booklet

A 12-page public introduction explaining the framework, system families, teaching use, and research direction.

Item Three

Teaching Packet

A worksheet-style packet for observing structure, cycles, divisions, mirrors, gates, and number patterns.
