ROSETTA WHEEL™ Printed Introductory Set

The first ROSETTA WHEEL™ printed proof sets are being prepared through Iannetti Studies.

ROSETTA WHEEL™ is a comparative symbolic systems framework focused on hidden structure, circular geometry, gates, mirrors, turns, number, and recurring organization across symbolic systems.

What is included
  • ROSETTA WHEEL™ Overview Brochure
  • ROSETTA WHEEL™ Public Introduction Booklet
  • ROSETTA WHEEL™ Symbolic Systems Teaching Packet
  • Presentation folder with printed front label
Status

Limited first-run printed proof sets are currently being assembled.

Once the proof sets arrive and are photographed, a small number may be made available directly through Iannetti Studies.

Use and scope

This printed introductory set is a public starting point. It does not disclose the full research archive, complete mappings, unpublished correspondence materials, or private working files.

The set is designed as printed publications and teaching materials in the field of symbolic systems.
